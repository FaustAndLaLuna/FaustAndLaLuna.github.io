Services

The King of Digital

Find Out Why

Services are a great way for companies to operate in the digital world.

Digital is a relatively new space that allows businesses to communicate with - not broadcast to - users and consumers. The internet also shares various characteristics with services, such as intangibility, inseparability and variability. Because of the large amount of investment both in money and in time, digital media has evolved to leverage the best of these characteristics and mitigate their problems. Finally, services permit companies to better work on their value offering and their sales funnel.

Communication

Digital allows users to talk with companies - both directly and indirectly. Communication is ideal for services to better adapt their service to its users. Direct communication takes the form of the usage of Customer Services and interactions through social media, while indirect communication takes the form of interaction with buttons, contact forms, carousels and accordions.

  • Intangibility

    Digital media and platforms are inherently intangible to the end user.  The Web 1.0 evolved  in order to allow users to store thoughts and experiences in ways that can be shared and can have a sort of permanency (Web 2.0 and 3.0), such as photos and videos shared in instagram that can add value to a service.

  • Inseparability

    The internet is time-based: web pages are updated in order to display user-generated content, to  better represent what the creator wants to communicate, and even digital products must keep up with their operating systems Digital platforms have a huge ecosystem for delivering services and consuming information such as the world wide web, intranets, cds, hard drives, ssds, etc. as well as platforms such as whatsapp, google drive, zoom, google meets, etc.

  • Variability

    Digital platforms generate a great amount of information that can be used to tailor experiences to their users. Everything can be measured and used for a service to change depending to the user's wants or needs. For example: if a video game player doesn't enjoy a certain game mode, they can choose not to play it, but if the game mode is widely unpopular, the developer company can change it to better adapt to the market
